Hey friends, happy to see you all again. Here is a set of information before you set to play

  • You get to see a deck of cards on the screen
  • Cute animals are hidden under these cards
  • You can flip two cards at a time to see the hidden animal
  • If it is same animal, then you got a go and that two cards are removed
  • If not, then try to remember that animal under that respective card, because this is a MEMORY game!
  • Continue flipping of your choice until you figure out the same animal
